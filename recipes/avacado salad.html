Avacado Salad

Back to Home

Description

This avocado salad is a delicious combination of ripe avocados, sweet onions, fresh tomatoes, and cilantro. This recipe is so easy to make and very colorful — I think you'll like it!

Ingredients

  • Avacado
  • Onion
  • Bell peper
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Combine avocados, onion, bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, and lime juice in a large bowl
  2. Gently toss until evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add cheese if you wish
